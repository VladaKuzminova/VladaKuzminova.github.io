Inquisitive and dilligent artist with a generalist skillset in 3D Animation, specifically dynamic simulations, character animation, modeling, surfacing and lighting. I am always eager to learn about new tools and techniques, and take on creative challenges in digital and traditional media. Throughout my 4 years at Sheridan College I had a chance to learn about and participate in every step of the animation pipeline, studying alongside some of the most creative and dedicated artists I know. I was one of the production managers in my 3rd year group film "Overthrone", which was the first 3D production I was involved in. A lot of the skills and technical knowledge I picked up during that time helped me to go on and and create my own short 3D film titled "Our Demons" in my last year at Sheridan.
